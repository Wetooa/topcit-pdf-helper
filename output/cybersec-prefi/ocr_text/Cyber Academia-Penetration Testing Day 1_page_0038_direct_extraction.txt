OWASP ZAP: Installation
ZAP has installer  for Windows  , Linux,  and 
MAC  OS/X . There  are also Docker  images  
available  on the download  site listed  
below .  
https ://www .zaproxy .org/download/
Install  ZAP on the system  you intended  to 
perform  pen testing  on
Download  the appropriate  installer  from  the 
download  page
NOTE  that ZAP requires  Java  8+ in order  to 
run.
Before  proceeding,  ensure  you have  
permission  from  web application  owner  to 
perform  a penetration  test.

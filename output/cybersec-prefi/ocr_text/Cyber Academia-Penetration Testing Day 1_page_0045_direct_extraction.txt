SQLMap
SQLMap  is an open -source 
penetration testing tool.
It automates the process of detecting 
and exploiting SQL injection flaws in 
a database.
It can detect and exploit SQL 
injection vulnerabilities, enumerate 
databases, tables, and columns, and 
access the operating system
It can also detect and crack password 
hashes.
It is highly customizable and has a 
variety of configuration options and 
settings.
SQLMap  requires knowledge of SQL 
injection vulnerabilities and SQL 
databases to be used effectively.

PENTESTING PROCESS:
1.Planning & Scoping : Define scope, rules, 
and objectives.
2.Reconnaissance : Gather information (IPs, 
ports, versions)
3.Vulnerability Scanning : Identify known 
weaknesses, vulnerabilities
4.Exploitation : Exploit identified 
vulnerabilities manually or with tools.
5.Post -exploitation : Assess the impact and 
potential access gained.
6.Reporting : Document findings and 
remediation recommendations.

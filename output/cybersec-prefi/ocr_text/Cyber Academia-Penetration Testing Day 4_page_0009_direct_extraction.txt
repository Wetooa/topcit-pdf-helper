Phases
Reconnaissance : The tester gathers information about the system and its 
environment
Privilege escalation : The tester attempts to gain higher levels of access to the 
system
Lateral movement : The tester moves laterally, attempting to gain access to other 
parts of the network or other systems that are connected to the compromised 
system. 
Data exfiltration : The tester attempts to extract sensitive information from the 
system
Covering tracks : The tester attempts to cover their tracks to avoid detection by 
system administrators or security teams.

SCOPE: What to Consider When 
Defining Scope
The types of systems to be tested in terms of 
whether they are on premise or cloud
Existing defenses and security controls, including 
gaps, vulnerabilities, attack responses, and 
current security postures
System, network, and device configurations
System, network, and device tolerance levels 
during an attack
Depth and breadth of attack (i.e., sophistication 
level) in order to compromise systems
The entire risk landscape and overall environment 
in which a threat actor may gain access

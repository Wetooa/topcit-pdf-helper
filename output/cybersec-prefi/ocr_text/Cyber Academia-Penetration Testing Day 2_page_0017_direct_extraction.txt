SCOPE and the Rules of Engagement
Define the rules of engagement in a written agreement that 
is signed by both parties.
Outline the testing methods and techniques that will be used 
during the engagement.
Specify the scope of the engagement, including any 
limitations or exclusions.
Identify any testing restrictions, such as prohibited actions or 
targets.
Define the level of access that the testing team will have to 
systems and data.
Define the communication and escalation procedures in case 
of any issues or incidents.
Define the confidentiality and data protection requirements 
for the engagement.
Identify the roles and responsibilities of both parties, 
including the testing team and the organization being tested.

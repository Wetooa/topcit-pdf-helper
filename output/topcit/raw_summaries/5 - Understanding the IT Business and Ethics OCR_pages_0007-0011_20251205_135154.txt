### V. Understanding the IT Business Process

#### 1. Component Elements for IT Business Process Execution

*   **People & Organization:** The role of human resources and organizational structure in executing IT processes.
*   **Business Process:** Define what a business process is in an IT context.

#### 2. Examples of IT Business Processes

*   Illustrate various types of IT business processes.

#### 3. Methods of Executing IT Business Processes

*   **IT Business Process Management (BPM):** Define BPM – managing and optimizing business processes using IT.
*   **IT Business Process Information Management:** How information is managed and flows within IT business processes.

#### 4. IT Business Process Improvement Techniques

*   **Business Process Reengineering (BPR):** Radical redesign of business processes.
*   **Process Innovation (PI):** Introducing new or significantly improved processes.
*   **Six Sigma:** A data-driven methodology for eliminating defects and improving quality.
*   **TRIZ (Theory of Inventive Problem Solving):** A problem-solving methodology based on observed patterns of invention.
*   **SERVQUAL:** A framework for measuring service quality.
*   **Statistical Process Control (SPC):** Using statistical methods to monitor and control a process.

### VI. Understanding IT Business Services

#### 1. General-Purpose Work Support Systems (Enterprise Solutions)

*   **Enterprise Solution:** Define software packages that support an organization's core business functions.
*   **Types of Enterprise Solutions:** List common categories.
*   **Business Strategy & Enterprise Solutions:** How these solutions support and enable business strategies.
*   **Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP):** Integrated management of core business processes.
*   **Supply Chain Management (SCM):** Managing the flow of goods and services.
*   **Customer Relationship Management (CRM):** Managing interactions with current and potential customers.
*   **Product Lifecycle Management (PLM):** Managing a product's entire lifecycle from conception to disposal.

#### 2. IT Business Decision-Making Support Systems

*   **Enterprise Data Warehouse (EDW):** A central repository of integrated data for reporting and analysis.
*   **Decision Support System (DSS):** Systems that aid in decision-making by analyzing data.
*   **Business Intelligence (BI):** Technologies and strategies for data analysis and presentation of actionable information.

#### 3. IT Service Management

*   **Overview of IT Service Management:** Define ITSM – managing the end-to-end delivery of IT services to customers.
*   **ITIL (Information Technology Infrastructure Library):** A widely adopted framework for ITSM.
*   **Service Level Agreement (SLA):** A contract outlining the level of service expected from a provider.
*   **Service Level Management (SLM):** Activities to ensure IT services meet agreed-upon service levels.

### VII. Understanding IT Business Performance Evaluation

#### 1. Financial Performance Evaluation

*   **Overview:** Assessing an organization's financial health and efficiency.
*   **Types of Financial Performance Evaluation Techniques:** Common methods like ratio analysis, ROI, payback period.

#### 2. Balanced Scorecard (BSC) Technique

*   **Overview of BSC:** A strategic performance management framework.
*   **Component Elements of BSC:** Key aspects included in a BSC.
*   **Four Perspectives of BSC:**
    *   **Financial:** How do we look to shareholders?
    *   **Customer:** How do customers see us?
    *   **Internal Business Process:** What must we excel at?
    *   **Learning & Growth:** Can we continue to improve and create value?

#### 3. Balanced IT Performance Evaluation Technique (IT-BSC)

*   **Overview of IT-BSC:** Adapting the BSC specifically for IT performance measurement.
*   **Component Elements of IT-BSC:** Specific metrics and perspectives for IT.

#### 4. Management of Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

*   **Overview of KPI:** Define Key Performance Indicators – measurable values demonstrating how effectively a company is achieving key business objectives.
*   **KPI Establishment Procedure:** Steps to identify, define, and implement KPIs.
*   **Precautions When Deriving KPI:** Considerations to ensure effective and meaningful KPIs.

#### 5. Goal Management System

*   Understand the concept and implementation of systems designed to set, track, and achieve organizational goals.

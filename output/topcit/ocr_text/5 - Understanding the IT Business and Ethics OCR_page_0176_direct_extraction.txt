>> Recent  Trends  and Major Issues
The development  of IT technology  is causing rapid fluctuations  in all industries  in the modern society,
maximizing  the convenience  and efficiency  of modern life. In particular,  the services  created by IT
technology  are being developed  to operate  prioritizing  the user's convenience  and interests,  but as the
enhanced  software  intelligence  and increases  the side effects and risks of misuse, abuse, and malicious
use. The importance  of ethical responsibility  is also increasing.  Against  this backdrop,  it is necessary  to
understand  the professional  ethics of IT business-related  developers.
>> Learning  Objectives:
1. To be able to describe  the professional  ethics of IT business  workers.
2. To be able to explain the principles  and codes of professional  ethics related  to IT business.
>> Keywords:
Professional  Ethics, Ethics, Work Ethics, Community  Ethics, Ethics and Law

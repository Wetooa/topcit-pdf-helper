>> Recent  Trends  and Major Issues
In the past, whether  the project was completed on schedule,  did not exceed the budget, and
achieved  the originally  planned  objective  were the criteria for measuring  its success and failure. In
contrast,  today, how much the project contributed  quantitatively  and qualitatively  to achieving  the
company's  management  goals and improvement  of competitiveness  is an evaluating  criterion.
>> Learning  Objectives
To be able to explain the concept  of project  evaluation  and step-by-step  process
>> Concept  summary
+ Evaluation  stage: Ex-ante  evaluation,  interim evaluation,  ex-post  evaluation
+ Evaluation  type: IT investment  performance  management,  performance  evaluation

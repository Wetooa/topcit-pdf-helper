Article 2 of Personal  Information  Protection  Act
The term "personal  information”  means any information  relating  to a living individual:  Information  that
identifies  a particular  individual  by his or her full name, resident  registration  number,  image, etc. (including
information  which, even if it by itself does not identify  a particular  individual,  may be easily combined  with
other information  to identify  a particular  individual.)

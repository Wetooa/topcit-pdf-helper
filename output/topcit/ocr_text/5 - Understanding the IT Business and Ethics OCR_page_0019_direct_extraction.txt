whose interdependence  with business  strategy  objectives  and processes  is maximized.  This maximization  of
interdependence  not only strengthens  the competitiveness  of a company's  business model but also plays a major
role in creating  new business  value. [Figure 3] shows the interdependence  between  a coprorate  business  and the IT
system  of a corporation.

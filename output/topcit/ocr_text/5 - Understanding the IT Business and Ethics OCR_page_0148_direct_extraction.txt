Cloud computing  is sub-category  of next-generation  computing.  It has been standardized  around cloud security,
cloud clients, and cloud platform  virtualization,  which means different  cloud services  are compatible  with each other.
In addition,  guidelines  for managing  quality indicators  needed for service operations  have been established  and are
the focus of a lot of effort because  such guidelines  are the key to stimulating  the growth  of the market.

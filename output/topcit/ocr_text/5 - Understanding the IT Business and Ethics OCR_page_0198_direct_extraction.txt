@ De-identification  Process  of Personal  Data
Businesses,  who want to process  information  for data analysis,  etc., should de-identify  and manage  the information  if
it is personal  information.
De-identification  refers to de-identifying  elements  that can identify  an individual  so that a specific individual  cannot
be identified  by processing  personal information  with alternative  methods  such as pseudonymization,  aggregate
processing,  deletion,  categorization,  and data masking.

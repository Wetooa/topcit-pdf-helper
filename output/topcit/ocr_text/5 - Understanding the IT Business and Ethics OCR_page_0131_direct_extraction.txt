The product  platform is a platform  of the common  parts used in the production  of a final product.  The purpose  of
such a platform  is to reduce costs. The customer  platform  is the core customer  group targeted  by a company  from
which more profits can be made. Finally, the trading  platform  is an infrastructure  that supports  a trading  relationship
with external  suppliers.  Through  customer  entrenchment  and cooperative  relationships,  an enterprise  can become  a
leading  player in the market  by leveragingits  trading  platform.
Many companies  try to create various business  models and build a stable business  ecosystem  based on their own
platforms.  As a result, it is reasonable  to predict that the business model will expand and the size of the platform
market  continue  to grow in the future, and that it could in fact see accelerated  growth.
@ API economy

programming  interface  or API and create additional  business  values through  the production  of new assets and
consumptions.  The series of activities  involved  in this effort are collectively  called “the API economy”.

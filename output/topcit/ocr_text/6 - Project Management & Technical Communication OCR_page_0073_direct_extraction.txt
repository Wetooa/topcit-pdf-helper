B) Responsibilities  and roles of project  manager
“Who is the project  manager,  and what does the person do on the project?”
The project manager  is the person responsible  for the entire scope and schedule  of the project. In other
words, the person oversees  and communicates  the progress  of the project, as well as resolving  issues and
risks to quality and stakeholders  from the initial stage to the end of the project.”  Project managers  need to
understand  and be able to exert the following three distinct  competencies  in order to carry out a project.

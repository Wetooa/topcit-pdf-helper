[Fig.8] The cyclical  management  of IT business
The cyclical management  structure  of IT business  is shown in [Fig, 8]. The IT service and  IT infra provide  operational
support  aimed at enablinga  business  (process)  toachieve  results in its IT business.
In IT business  management,  new IT business  performance  indicators  are derived from the service quality of the IT
operational  support  service (IT QoS) and the business  performance  results of the organization.  These indicators  are
then sent to the IT division.  The IT business  management  provides  support  for the making  and adjusting  of decisions,
and investments,  to the IT service and  IT infra,thus  ensuring  that the target levels for the performance  indicators  can
be met.

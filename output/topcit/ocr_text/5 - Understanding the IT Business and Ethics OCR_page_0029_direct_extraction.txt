>> Recent  Trends  and Major Issues
The development  of a company’s  IT business  strategy  is the process  of finding the most effective  way
of achieving  its mission and objectives  under the constraints  of the internal and external  environments
to which it is subject.  Setting  the right strategy  is particularly critical for this new concept  of IT business,
which involves  the integration  of IT and business  processes.  As the domain of IT business  continues  to
expand,  and the business  process  becomes  ever more closely integrated  with IT, interest  is growing  in how
to establish  IT business  strategies  and find the right way to analyze  the environment  before setting  one’s
strategies.  This section introduces  the current  model and the future model, which are essential  elements
in developing  IT business  strategies,  as well as the strategies  for implementing  them, and also presents
different  methods  of analyzing  the IT business  environment  (including  SWOT  analysis).
>> Learning  Objectives
1. To be able to explain  the IT business  strategy.
2. To be able to explain the business  environment  analysis  model for formulating  IT business  strategies.
>> Keywords
IT business  strategy  formulation,  IT business  environment  analysis

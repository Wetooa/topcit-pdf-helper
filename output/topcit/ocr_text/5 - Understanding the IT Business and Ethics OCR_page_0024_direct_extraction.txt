03 IT Resources  and Management  Activities  for IT Business
A) The concept  of IT resources
The concept  of “IT resources”  encompasses  the people, processes,  software  and hardware  used to run anIT business.
IT resources  include IT support  systems  and IT management  systems,  of which the former are systems  that support
or manage IT businesses,  such as ERP (Enterprise  Resource Management),  SCM (Supply  Chain Management)and
PLM(Product  Lifecycle  Management),  while the latter IT typically  include information  technology  service  management
(ITSM).
IT resources  evolve continuously  while maintaining  a direct relationship  with the IT business  in question.  They are
changing  in a direction  that maximizes  the results of an IT business  with changing  technologies  and the changing
times.
The system for managing  IT resources  is called an IT resource  management  system: Essentially,  it manages  and
controls  the solutions,  products,  and processes  required to support  IT businesses.  The IT resource  management
system manages  resources  (from planning  and development  through application,  operation  and management  to
disposal)  using the IT resource  lifecycle as a template.  It manages  the specification  list of IT resources  as well as the
gains in business  performance  achieved  by utilizing  IT resources.

>> Recent  trends and Major Issues
With the revolution  of the IT industry,  new products  and services  are being developed  across industries
through the combination/convergence  of technologies.  However,  while using the latest products  and
services,  technical  competence  is improving,  on the contrary,  various  side-effects  such as safety accidents,
information  leakage, and invasion  of privacy are increasing  due to the lack of ethical awareness  and
understanding  of ethics. In addition,  these downsides  are a factor that hinders the development  of the
IT business. Therefore,  in order to continue  the development  of IT business  services,  it is necessary  to
examine  ethical issues in each service  field and establish  a correct  ethical awareness.
>> Learning  Objectives
1.To be able to explain  the ethical issues of the latest IT business  services.
2.To be able to describe  ethical compliance  in the latest IT business  services.
>> Keywords

Understanding  the IT Business  and Ethics
>> Recent  trends and key issues
In recent  years, the IT business  environment  has been transformed  rapidly  with smart mobile as the base.
This is a transformation  that has put IT in a position  where it can lead business  as the core service of
many different  business  models. The role of IT is no longer limited to just supporting  business.  Rather, IT
is increasingly  accelerating  the transformation  of the business  environment  as mobile, open-source, and
social services  are combined  with infrastructures  such as cloud, big data, loT, and Al.
>> Learning  Objectives
1. To be able to explain  the trend in the transformation  of the IT business  environment.
2. To be able to explain enterprise  activities  for responding  to transformations  of the IT business  environment.
>> Keywords
Disruptive  innovation,  crowd sourcing,  open innovation,  business  platform,  API economy,  servitization

@ Planning  stage
The planning  stage involves  linking business  with IT, setting  the goals of IT, establishing  the structures,  activities  and
mechanisms  regarding  the rules on organizing  IT related activities,  using resources,  managing  risks, the governance
structure,  and measuring  performance.
@ Operations  stage
The monitoring  of IT resources,  IT risks and IT management  involves  all the structures,  activities  and related
mechanisms  for helping companies  to monitor  their IT resources,  IT risks and IT management.  The monitoring  of
IT performance  measurement  involves  the structures,  activities  and related mechanisms  for helping  companies  to
set target levels (for their performances  related to IT resources,  IT risks and IT management)  and measure  their
attainment  of such targets.
@ Evaluation  stage
IT value is evaluated  through  the eamings  and costs of the future business  opportunities  delivered  to the company.
IT value includes  strategic,  economic,  and risk management,  as well as technical,  social and quality  benefits.  To arrive
at the IT value, the costs of business  risk and other potential  risk costs are subtracted  from business  opportunities.

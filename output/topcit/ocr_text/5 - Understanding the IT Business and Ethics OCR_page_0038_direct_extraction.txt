B) Internal  Environment  Analysis
@ 7S Analysis  Method
The 7S model used to analyze  a company’s  internal environment  is an internal environmental  analysis technique
created by the McKinsey  consulting  firm. This is a method of diagnosing  a company  by identifying  its core
competency  components.  It defines  the core competency  components  of a company  in terms of 7 items that start
with the letter S.
Strategy
+ Shared  Values
The ideals and mission shared by members  of an organization  or a company  must be analyzed.  Find the values
emphasized  within the company  and ascertain  whether  the values are universally  shared among  the members,
+ Strategy
Analyze  the company’s  strategy  on its goals and vision, assess the budget, organization,  and human resources
needed  for realizing  the company’s  strategy.
+ System
Analyze the process  or system (technique)  for managing  people and making important  decisions.  Regulations  and
policies  are all included  in the scope of a system.
+ Structure

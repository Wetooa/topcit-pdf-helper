F) Understanding  SPC
© Overview  of SPC
SPC stands for “Statistical  Process  Control’:  This term contains  the word ‘statistical’  because  it relies on statistical
data and analysis  techniques;  the word ‘process’  because  it represents  a given product  quality specification  and
the capacity  of the process; and ‘control’  because  the technique  allows enterprises  to control the condition  of the
product  when it is produced.
+ SPC is a management  technique  that allows enterprises  to make repeated  improvements  to a process  and maintain
high rates of production  of goods of satisfactory  quality.
+ SPC allows enterprises  to conduct  an engineering  analysis  of the causes of defects  and establish  the best way of
rectifying  the problem  in a reasonable  manner.

01 Information  System  Structure
A) Concept  of information  system
An information  system uses information  technology  (IT) to perform  business  procedures,  such as providing  necessary
information, or business  processing  required  by enterprises.  It creates a system that enables  IT personnel  to provide
IT services  by using IT components,  such as hardware  and software,  to perform  organizational  business  procedures.
As shown in [Figure 75], the information  system provides  services  by using the technology  resources  of the IT
infrastructure.  The IT infrastructure  includes  computing  platforms,  such as networks,  hardware,  software,  IT personnel,
and IT services.

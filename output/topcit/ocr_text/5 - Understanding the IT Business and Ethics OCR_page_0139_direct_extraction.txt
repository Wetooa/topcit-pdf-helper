@ SNS(Social  Network  Service)
Social Network  Service (SNS) refers to a service that helps people continue  their personal  relationships  online or to
establish  new relationships.  Early SNSs began in the form of online communities  on websites,  such as "The Well”
which began in 1985, and "Theglob.com,”  which started in 1994. These are the first examples  of SNS that provided
services  in the form of early communities.

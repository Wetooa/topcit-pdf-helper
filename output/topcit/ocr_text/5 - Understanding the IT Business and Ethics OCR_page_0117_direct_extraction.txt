04 Balanced  IT Performance  Evaluation  Technique
A) Overview  of IT-BSC
IT-BSC (IT-Balanced  Score Card) is a performance  management  tool that helps enterprises  to achieve  their goals by
introducing  the concept  of BSC in the evaluation  of investment  performance  in the IT sector.
The growing  importance  of IT in corporate  activities  has led enterprises  to recognize  the need for a method  of
systematically  evaluating  IT investments. With the traditional  finance-based  investment  evaluation  technique,  it is
difficult  to quantify  intangible  IT investments,  and hence enterprises  have rushed to adopt IT-BSC.

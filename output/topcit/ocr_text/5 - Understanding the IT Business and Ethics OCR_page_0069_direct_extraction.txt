A) IT business  process  management
BPM is a key technique  of IT business  process management  that consists  in optimizing  and automating  business
processes.  In addition,  BAM is a technique  for discovering  and managing  bottlenecks  in business  processes,  while BRE
is a business  rule engine  that sets and manages  the flow of business  processes.

@ Understanding  BPM
Business  process  management  (BPM) is a technique  for the consolidated  management,  optimization  and automation
of the flow of people, information  resources,  and tasks associated  with IT business  processes,  It is also an important
means  of implementing  IT business  processes.  How well the overall process  is managed  can have direct repercussions
for business  performance.  Hence, it is essential  to take a logical and structured  approach  to managing  the IT business
process.  BPM must be capable  of handling  changes  in the management  environment  and should be designed  to be
capable  of responding  actively  to changes  in business  processes.

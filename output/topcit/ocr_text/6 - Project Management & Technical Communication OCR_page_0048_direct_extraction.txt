02 Principles  and Methods  of Writing  Technical  Documents
A) Expression  and description  methods  of writing  technical  documents
Technical  documents  are documents  that must have accuracy  and logical validity, and they emphasize  strict
logic and format compared  to other documents.  Therefore,  it is important  to be faithful to the rules of lan-
guage and formal logic, to make sure there are no errors. You should pay attention  to the following  numbering
system,  commonly  misspelled  foreign words, and Korean spelling.

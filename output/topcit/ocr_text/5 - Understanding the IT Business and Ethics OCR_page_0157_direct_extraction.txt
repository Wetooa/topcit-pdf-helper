[Fig.68]  Smart  traffic service  classification
+ V2V service
The V2V service is a service in which vehicles,  the main agents of the traffic system, exchange  information  via
wireless  communication  with their surrounding  vehicles.  The service allows vehicles  to send/receive  messages  about
their positions,  traffic flow information,  and road traffic information.  Major V2V services  include roadside  information
and obstacle  notification  services,  road construction  notification  services,  intersection  traffic signal notification
services,  nearby  vehicle  information  notification  services,  and emergency  vehicle  access  notification  services.

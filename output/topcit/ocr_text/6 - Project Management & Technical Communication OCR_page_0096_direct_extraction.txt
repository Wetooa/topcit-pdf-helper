Schedule  control is a process  of changing  and managing  the schedule  of a project  while monitoring  the status of project
activities  to accomplish  the plan.
Schedule  control focuses  on the following:
+ Determine  the current  status of the project  schedule
+ Adjustment  of factors  causing  schedule  change
+ Determine  whether  the project  schedule  has changed
+ Management  of changes  that actually  occur
In an agile project  methodology,  schedule  control  focuses  on the following:
+ Determine  the current status of the project schedule  by comparing  the total amount  of work received  and

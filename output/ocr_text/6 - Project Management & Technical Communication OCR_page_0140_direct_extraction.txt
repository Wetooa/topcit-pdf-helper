Project managers  should check the project plan and Business  Case to determine  quantitatively  whether  the
project has reached  its goals, through project management  tools and evaluation,  thereby  actively  managing
and supporting  continuous  process  improvement.
C) Project  evaluation  stage and focal point
The project  evaluation  stage is a task that analyzes  how much the IT project  has contributed  to the achieve-
ment of business  goals from the three points of view previously  examined.  It can be divided into Ex-ante  eval-
uation, Interim evaluation,  and Ex-post  evaluation  depending  on when the project  is evaluated.

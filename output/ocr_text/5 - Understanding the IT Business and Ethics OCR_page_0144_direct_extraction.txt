platform  to provide a service  that monitors  the status of objects,  controls  the situation  and sends information  about
the status of objects to users. Network  communication  networks  are used to provide people or intelligent  devices
with information  about  objects  or to let them control the objects.  Typical  types of M2M services  are described  below.

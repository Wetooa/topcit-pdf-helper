@ Necessity  of personal  information  protection
+ The emergence  of public and private services  in which personal  information  is used in combination  with information
technology.
+ Personal  information  is not just information  but becomes  an asset of a company  such as electronic  civil service for
public institutions,  education,  finance,  information  and communication,  medical  care, entertainment,  etc.
* If personal  information  is leaked, it can be abused  for crime and fraud through  illegal trafficking  and impersonation.
+ Increasing  personal  information  infringement  accidents  and class action lawsuits  by victims  of leakage  cause loss of
corporate  management.
+ Protection of  personal  information  becomes  an essential  element  of national and social safety and corporate
development.
+ Personal  information  and privacy  protection,  which has become  a global trend, serves as a growth  engine of Korea.
+ Personal  information  protection  becomes  an important  issue in industries.
+ The signing  of FTAs with countries  around the world has increased  cross-border  exchange  of personal  information.
+ Personal  information  issues affect public administration  credibility  and national  brand.

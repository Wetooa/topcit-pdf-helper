The Five Forces Framework  proposed  by Michael Porter is a type of industry  structure  analysis  model. In a 1979 HBR
paper, he asserted  that the five factors shape the structure  of an industry,  and that companies  can actively  respond
to threats  by closely  studying  the five factors.
@ 3C and 4P Analysis  Method
The 3C/4P framework,  a micro-external environmental  method  of analysis, issued to analyze  the market of the
industry  to which a company  belongs.  It is widely used for marketing  products  and business  services  and for analyzing
the competitive  market  environment.
+ 3C -Customer,  Company,  Competition
+ 4P -Product,  Price, Place, Promotion
The 3C’s consist  of three pillars: customer  analysis,  company  analysis,  and competitor  analysis.  In customer  analysis,
the market  size and market  growth rate are estimated  by identifying  the customer  target, product  positioning,  and
customer  demand  hierarchy.
In company  analysis,  the internal/external  environment  surrounding  the company,  as well as its strengths  and
weaknesses, are examined  (in conjunction  with the SWOT analysis  technique) and used  in setting the company’s
goals and developing  its strategies.
In competitor  analysis,  current  competitors  and potential  competitors  are identified  by using market  analysis  and the
competitors’  financial  data and business  portfolios.
The 4P’s consist  of product,  price, place (place of sale), and promotion.
In product  analysis,  the design, brand, characteristics,  brand power, performance,  and quality  of the product  concerned
are analyzed.
In price analysis,  the product  price, discount  condition,  and demand  as a function  of price levels are analyzed,  and the
price is compared  to the prices of the competitors,  and the price flexibility  is examined.
In place analysis,  the region of product  sales, distribution,  logistics,  and inventory  levels are analyzed.
In promotion  analysis,the  advertising,  promotion,  marketing,  and customer  communication  strategies  are analyzed.

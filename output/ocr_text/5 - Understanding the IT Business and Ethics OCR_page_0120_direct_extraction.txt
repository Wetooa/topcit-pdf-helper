B) The main reasons  why enterprises  are adopting  MBO
MBO begins with a clear understanding  of an enterprise's  objectives  and makes each individual  responsible  for
meeting  their own individual  goals. Also, under the MBO system,  efficient  organization  management  can be secured
since the members  of the organization  work hard to achieve  their respective  goals.

C) Component  elements  of MBO
From the very goal setting stage, MBO encourages  the participation  of supervisors  and subordinates.  Therefore,  the
MBO approach  works interactively  to align the goals with the enterprise  strategies.  In addition,  continuous  feedback  in
the form of evaluations  and encouragement  is needed  to align the MBO of employees  to the goals of the enterprise.

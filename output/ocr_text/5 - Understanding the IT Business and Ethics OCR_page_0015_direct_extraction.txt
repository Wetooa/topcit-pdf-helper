>> Recent  Trends  and Major Issues
In the past, the perception  of IT among the business  community  was that IT could play an auxiliary  role
in business  processes,  but that nothing more could be expected  from it. However,  as digital technologies
have become  ever more deeply  embedded  in usersâ€™ lifestyles,  IT is being actively  integrated  into corporate
business  processes.  It is from this perspective  that people must understand  not only the concept  of (IT?)
business  but also the IT ecosystem  and IT value chain related  to IT business.
>> Learning  Objectives
1. To be able to explain  the concept  of IT business  and its characteristics.
2. To be able to explain the IT business  ecosystem.
3. To be able to explain the business  value chain after understanding  the IT resources  allocated  to
businesses  and the activities  (roles/functions?)  of such IT resources.
>> Keywords
Concept  of IT business,  IT business  ecosystem,  IT business  value chain

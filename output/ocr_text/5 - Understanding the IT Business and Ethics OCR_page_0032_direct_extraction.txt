B) The elements  of a strategy  plan
There are many different  approaches  to choose  from when developing  a strategy.  At a typical strategy  development
stage, the current conditions  of the subject company  are evaluated,  future goals are set, and implementation
strategies  for realizing  the company’s  target model are formulated.

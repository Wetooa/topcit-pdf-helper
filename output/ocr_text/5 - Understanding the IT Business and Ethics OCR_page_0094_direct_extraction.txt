In CRM, the customer  point of contact  has huge significance.  The customer  point of contact  or channel  collects  the
customer's  VoC and communicates  the results produced  by the internal process  to the customer.  Therefore,  when
the component  elements  of CRM must be identified,  it is advisable  to investigate  the channel  first of all, since this is
the point of contact  with the customers.  Also, if information  analysis  is conducted,  followed  by information  utilization,
it becomes  very easy to understand  the constituent  elements  of CRM.

MDM is a solution  for efficiently  managing  all the diverse mobile devices and applications  used in enterprise
operations.  It is a system that can be used to manage  mobile devices  anytime,  anywhere  through  over-the-air  (OTA)
transmissions  (although  the device must be in the power  on state).

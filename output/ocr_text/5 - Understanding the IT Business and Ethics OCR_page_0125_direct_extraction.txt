@ Disruptive  innovation

depression  in the business  market. Disruptive  innovation  is a technique  for destroying  existing  markets  and creating
new markets.In  essence,  it consists  in dominating  the market with technological  innovation/price  destruction  or in
pursuing  a business  strategy  aimed at beating  the market  leaders.
Disruptive  innovation  is an innovation  technology  that can alter the hierarchy  of competition  in existing  industries,

E) Requirement  traceability  matrix
The Requirement  Traceability  Matrix is a document  that tracks the process  of the user's requirements  to be
reflected  in the final deliverable.  Through  this document,  it is possible  to trace how the user's requirements
are reflected  in the product,  and trace back to what requirements  the implemented  product  is based on.
In the document,  WBS, design, developed  product,  test scenario,  etc. are mapped  and managed  with user re-
quirements.

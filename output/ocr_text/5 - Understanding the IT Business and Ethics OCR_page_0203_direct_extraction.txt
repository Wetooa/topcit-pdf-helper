B) Follow-up  measures  for personal  information  controllers
@ Response  to personal  information  leakage
If personal  information  is leaked (mandatory  obligation in case of more than one thousand  data subjects  - Article 34
of the Personal  Information  Protection  Act, Article 39 of the Enforcement  Decree  of the same Act), the data subjects
must be notified immediately  and actions such as confirmation  of facts, blocking  of the website,  notification  of
password  change,  etc. must be promptly  initiated.
In addition,  victims of leakage  may file collective  dispute mediation  with the Dispute Mediation  Committee  or file a
lawsuit  against  the infringement  of rights to the court, which requires  thorough  preparation.
@ Notification  of personal  information  leakage
If personal information  over a certain scale is leaked, it is required  to report to specialized  agencies  such as the
Ministry  of Security  and Public Administration  and the Korea Internet  & Security  Agency  (KISA) to provide support  for
damage  prevention  and recovery  technology.

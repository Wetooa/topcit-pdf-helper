03 IT Business  Adoption  Method
There are two distinct methods  of adopting  an IT business:  in-house  development  and package  purchase.  The
advantage  of in-house  development  is that it allows a company  to fully reflect the needs of users by utilizing  its own
internal personnel.  A purchased  package  has the advantage  of being based on a standardized  advanced  process
which is conducive  to improving  work efficiency.
@ Characteristics  of in-house  development
This is a form of adoption  that allows companies  to set up and develop  information  systems  using their own
manpower,  resulting  in a system  that is faithful to the internal  requirements  of the company.  While know-how  can be
accumulated  with this method,  it involves  relatively  higher adoption  costs, while success  cannot  be guaranteed.
@ Characteristics  of package  purchase
This is a form of adoption  under which a company  can easily absorb the know-how  gained from early cases of IT
business  adoption.  By adopting  a system that has undergone  many tests and adoption  verifications,  uncertainty
about success  can be eliminated. The adoption  period is short and the cost is relatively  low, but the maintenance
costs are likely to increase

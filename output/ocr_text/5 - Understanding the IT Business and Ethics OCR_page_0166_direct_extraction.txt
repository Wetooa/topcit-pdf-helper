In overseas,  the fintech industry  is growing  by leaps and bounds.  But the domestic  fintech industry  has failed to fully
blossom,  although  a market around mobile payment  settlement  services  and P2P loan services  has been formed.
There are many difficulties  to the growth of the domestic  fintech industry  because  there are only a few kinds of
businesses  that could be started  independently  without a partnership  between  financial institutions  and many
financial-related  regulations.  There are also too many regulations.  The government  needs to strengthen  regulations
and enforce  them in an impartial  manner  so that the fintech industry  could be energized  and generate  added values.

E) Understanding  SERVQUAL
@ Overview  of SERVQUAL
SERVQUAL  is a technique  for analyzing  the gap between  the level of service  quality  expected  by users and the actual
level of service  quality provided  by an enterprise.
+ Using the following  five dimensions,  SERVQUAL  prioritizes  the relative importance  (as felt by the user) of service
attributes,  thus making  it possible  for an enterprise  to provide  a service  based on its scale of priority.
+ SERVQUAL  was first proposed  by Parasuraman,  Zeithaml,  and Berry in 1985.

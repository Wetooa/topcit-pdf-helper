VI. Presentation
>> Recent  trends and major issues
Presentations  have the most important  influence  on winning  development  projects,  attracting
investment  in new product  development,  securing  decision-making  within the organization,  and
project  termination.  In many cases, success  or failure depends  on the presentation.  Along with writing
skills, presentation  skills are very important  in securing  decision-making  and support  for projects.

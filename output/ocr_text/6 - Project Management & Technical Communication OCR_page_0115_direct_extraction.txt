>> Recent  Trends  and Major Issues
Software  is getting more complex  due to the construction  of a large and highly functional  system.
Along with problems  such as delays in delivery  of software  construction  projects,  lower customer
satisfaction,  and excessive  cost for system operation/maintenance,  awareness  of quality is becoming
more important.
>> Learning  Objectives
1. To be able to explain the concept  and process  of quality  management.
2. To be able to describe  quality  standards  and evaluation  perspectives.
3. To be able to explain  quality  assurance  and quality control.
>> Keywords
* Quality management  process:  quality management  planning,  quality assurance,  quality control,
PDCA
* Quality  standards:  ISO/IEC  9126, ISO/IEC  12207, CMMI, 6 Sigma, ISO 9000
* Quality evaluation  perspective:  product  quality perspective,  process  quality perspective,  project
quality  perspective

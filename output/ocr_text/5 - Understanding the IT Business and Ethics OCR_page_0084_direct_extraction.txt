Enterprise  solutions  are used as a means  of maximizing  profits by shaving  time off business
operations  and reducing  costs.
Enterprise  solutions  function  as enablers  for work process  innovations.  They are essential  for the
rebuilding  of business  processes.
Enterprises  apply information  technologies  to their product  quality improvement  activities.
Items in need of quality  improvements  are identified  based on sales data and customer  data.
Enterprise  solutions  enable companies  to secure a loyal customer  base through  customer
relationship  management  and realize cost reductions  and the smooth  delivery  of products  by
continuously  improving  relationships  with suppliers.
Enterprise  solutions  provide  information  to executives  who need to make the right business
decisions  in a timely manner  by giving  them access  to real-time  market  data.
Enterprise  solutions  increase  the likelihood  of achieving  business  objectives,  including  excellence  in
enterprise  operations,  the development  of new products  and business  models,  the strengthening  of
customer/vendor  relationships,  and decision-making  improvements.
Through  technologies  like CAD/CAM,  enterprise  solutions  enable companies  to gain early dominance
of their markets  by helping  them reduce their product  development  times and innovate  their
engineering  processes  using IT.

C) Components  of an IT business  ecosystem
The table below describes  the characteristics  of the 5 components  (contents,  platform,  network,  device, and security)
that constitute  an IT ecosystem.
<Table2>  Components  of an IT business  ecosystem
Components Main characteristics
Contents  (C)Advances  in mobile technology  lead to increased  consumption  of mobile contents.
+ Amobile  environment  centered  on a smartphone  with computing  power  allows users to use
diverse  contents  free from the restrictions  of time and space.
+ In the new mobile environment,  users can not only enjoy music and video but also use
e-books  and other materials  with ease. An ecosystem  comprising  a new form of content
distribution  in which mobile users are the chief customer  segment  is gradually  gaining
acceptance
Platform  (P)IIT service platforms  are evolving  from individual  services  with a single operator  to a common
platform  in which multiple  providers  participate  together.
+ The closed platform  is being superseded  by the open platform.
+ The trend is changing  from building  one’s own platform  and providing  one’s own service  to
providing  services  using a common  platform  shared by a select few.
+ Apple, Google,  and Kakao have developed  their service  framework  based on the concept  of a
platform  in whichmany  operators  can participate.
Network  (N)The smart revolution  began with tablet PCs, smart TVs, and smartphones,  but is it now raising
the standard  of network  traffic bothqualitatively  and quantitatively.
+ Next-generation  smart services  such as smart homes and smart cars can generate  many
different  forms of large volume  traffic.
+ The diverse  types of loT devices  and sensors  used in thehyper-connected  society  are also
generating  massive  amounts  of data traffic.
Device  (D)The market  launching  of wearable  devices  has resulted  in fiercer  competition  as well as
collaboration  among  device makers.
+ Watch-type  products  such as Samsung's  Galaxy  Gear and goggle-type  products  such as the
Google  Glass are becoming  commercially  successful  products.
+ The number  of smartphones  and tablet PCs that can be connected  to smart devices  is
increasing.

04 Network  Service  Quality
A) Quality  of Service  (QoS) characteristics
QoS is the technology  of guaranteeing  a service quality and performance  of a communication  service  to meet user
requirements,  The attributes  to determine  the QoS include confidence,  delay, jitter, and bandwidth,  as shown in
<Table  45>.
{Table 45) QoS Measurements
Attribute Description
Confidence - An attribute  needed by a flow in order to deliver a packet safely to the destination
Delay - Adelay  when transmitting  a packet from the transmitting  side to the receiving  side
Jitter - Variation  of delay for packets belonging  to the same flow
Bandwidth - The maximum  rate available  in communication,  i.e. the ability to transmit  information

G) SANS (SysAdmin,  Audit, Networking,  and Security)  Top 25
SANS top 25 is the list of the most serious programming  errors that cause software  vulnerabilities  in three
categories,  including  the management  of unsafe and dangerous  resources  between  components,  and content
related to the use of inappropriate  security  countermeasures.  This list is created by the MITRE and SANS
associations,  based on CWE.

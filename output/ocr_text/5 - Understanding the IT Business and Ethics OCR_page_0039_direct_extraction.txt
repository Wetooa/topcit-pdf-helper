C) Consolidated  Internal  and External  Environment  Analysis
@ SWOT  Analysis
Generally  speaking,  SWOT analysis  has primarily  been used to establish  strategies  related to a company’s  marketing
activities,  although  Universities  also rely on SWOT analysis  to teach marketing  to students.  Today, however,  SWOT
analysis is used in a variety of ways beyond the realm of marketing  activities,  and is used by companies  as an
important  analytical  framework,  especially  when setting  a company’s  overall strategy.
SWOT analysis is a framework  for analyzing  opportunities  and threat factors in an external market environment
that could affect a company’s  core products  and services,  together  with its internal strengths  and weaknesses.
The results of the two analyses  are then matrixed.  Many companies  today use this framed matrix as a method  for
environmental  research  because  it provides  an important  basis for deriving  insights  into their strategic  directions.

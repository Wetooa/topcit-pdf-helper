C) Configuration  management  system,  CMS
Configuration  management  tool is a management  tool that systematically  manages  source codes or deliver-
ables in an environment  where several people work at the same time to prevent  duplicate  revisions  or loss of
updated  contents.  It mainly tracks the history of source codes or deliverables  and performs  version control.
The most popular  tools include CVS, Subversion,  and Git.

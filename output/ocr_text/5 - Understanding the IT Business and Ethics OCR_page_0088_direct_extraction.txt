@ ERP deployment  strategy
There are three approaches  to ERP deployment.  With the Big Bang method,  all modules  and functions  of the ERP.
system  are deployed  across the place of business  at the same time. In the phased  and function-based  approach,  the
ERP system modules  are separated  by their functions  and the ERP system is deployed  only on some modules.  In the
phased and place of business  approach,  the ERP system is deployed  in phases at each place of business.  When the
operation  of ERP is shown to be stable, its deployment  is then expanded  horizontally  through  the remaining  phases.

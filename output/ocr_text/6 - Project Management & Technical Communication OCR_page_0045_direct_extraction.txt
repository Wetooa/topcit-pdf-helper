>> Recent  Trends  and Major Issues
As we enter the information  society, document  exchange  using e-mail and decision  making
using electronic  approval  systems  are spreading.  As various project proposals,  reports, and work
performance  plans are prepared  frequently,  the ability to write logical and convincing  technical
documents  has become  a decisive  factor in determining  success  and failure in one's work.
>> Learning  Objectives
1. To be able to explain the concepts  and types of technical  documents.
2. To be able to clearly write technical  documents  that fit the purpose.

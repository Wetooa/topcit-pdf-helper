+ V2N service
A V2N service is a service  that lets a vehicle communicate  through  a network  with a server  or mobile device in the cloud
or on the Internet.  The TCU (Telecommunication  Control Unit) is installed  in the vehicle and communicates  with the
backend  server, which communicates  with the mobile  device to provide  information  to the user and controls  the vehicle.

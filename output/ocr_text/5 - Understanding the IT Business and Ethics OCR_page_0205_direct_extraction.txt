>> Recent  Trends  and Major Issues
Intellectual  property  rights consist  of all property  rights arising  from intellectual  activities,  industrial  property
rights for the purpose  of industrial  development,  and copyrights  for the purpose  of cultural development.
With recent economic,  social, or cultural changes  or the development  of science  and technology,  various
intellectual  properties  are also emerging  in new fields.
Intellectual  property  is an important  corporate  asset, and the transaction  of intellectual  property  is
exploding.  Therefore,  by learning  about intellectual  property  rights that you must know while working  in the
IT business  field, you can understand  the importance  of intellectual  property  protection.
>> Learning  Objectives
1. To be able to explain about copyrights  and copyrights  infringement  prevention.
2. To be able to explain intellectual  property  rights and industrial  property  rights.

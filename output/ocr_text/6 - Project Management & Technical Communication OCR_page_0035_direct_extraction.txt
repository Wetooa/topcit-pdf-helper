© Date and Organizer  (author)  of Report
The date and organizer  (author)  of a report should be indicated  at the right bottom of the title. This is
important  because  it provides  a basis for identifying  the document  later and prevents  confusion  with older
versions.  Beginners  often make the mistake  of omitting  this part.

should be prioritized,  and if there is none, choose  what you use the most. The following  numbering  system is
used a lot for general  plans.

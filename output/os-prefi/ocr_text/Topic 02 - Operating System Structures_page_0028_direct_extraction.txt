Process Management
These commands allow you monitor and terminate processes
•$ ps aux – Lists active processes.
•a – Show all users' processes
•u – Show detailed user info
•x – Show processes without a terminal
•$ top – Shows real-time system stats.
•Navigation:
•q – Quit.
•k – Kill a process .
•M – Sort by memory.
•$ kill 1234 – Stops a process by PID.
•-9 – Force kill.
•-15 – Graceful kill.
